Hello!

I'm dvkr, a full stack dev, and I write here.

The blog's name comes from hearing lots of recommendations on what to do or learn, and comparatively little in the way of guiding the execution. It's also a nod to a blog I enjoy reading very much: Wait, But Why? by Tim Urban.

I usually figure out what to do here by answering one of these questions:

  • How can [X] be better? How can it be fun?
  • How can we improve the 'tried-and-true' language or analogies used to explain something?
  • How do we address the challenges of new devs, especially from non-traditional backgrounds?

This blog is a quiet corner of an otherwise bustling internet, but I'd love to hear from you! You can find me on twitter.
